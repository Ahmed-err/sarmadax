## 1. Purpose & Scope

This Agreement establishes the general terms and conditions that govern all professional services provided by Sarmadax to Client. The specific scope, deliverables, timeline, and fees for each project are defined in a separate Statement of Work ("SOW") that references this Agreement. In the event of a conflict between this Agreement and any SOW, the SOW controls with respect to the specific project only.

## 2. Services

Sarmadax will provide the services described in each mutually executed SOW ("Services"). Sarmadax reserves the right to determine the manner and means by which Services are performed, provided that the final deliverables meet the requirements in the applicable SOW.

## 3. Payment Terms

### 3.1 Fees

All fees are set out in the applicable SOW. Sarmadax's standard payment structure is:

- **50% deposit** due before work begins
- **50% final payment** due upon project completion and delivery

Alternative milestone-based schedules may be agreed upon in writing within the SOW.

### 3.2 Invoicing

Sarmadax will issue invoices in accordance with the payment schedule in the SOW. Invoices are due within **7 days** of the invoice date unless otherwise stated in the SOW.

### 3.3 Late Payments

Invoices unpaid after the due date accrue interest at **1.5% per month** (18% per annum) on the outstanding balance. Sarmadax may suspend work on any active project until overdue invoices are settled.

### 3.4 Expenses

Unless included in the SOW, out-of-pocket expenses (e.g., third-party licenses, hosting, stock assets, stock photography) require prior written approval from Client and will be invoiced at cost with no markup.

### 3.5 Taxes

Client is responsible for any applicable taxes, duties, or levies imposed on the Services except for taxes on Sarmadax's income.

## 4. Intellectual Property

### 4.1 Assignment on Full Payment

Upon receipt of all payments due under the applicable SOW, Sarmadax assigns to Client all right, title, and interest — including copyright — in the final deliverables created specifically for Client under that SOW ("Work Product"), to the extent such deliverables qualify as original works of authorship.

### 4.2 Pre-existing Work & Tools

Sarmadax retains full ownership of all tools, frameworks, libraries, code bases, methodologies, and pre-existing intellectual property used in delivering the Services ("Background IP"). To the extent that Background IP is incorporated into the Work Product, Sarmadax grants Client a perpetual, non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free license to use such Background IP solely as incorporated in and necessary to use the Work Product.

### 4.3 Open-Source Components

The Work Product may include open-source software. Such components remain subject to their respective open-source licenses, which will be disclosed in the project delivery documentation.

### 4.4 Portfolio Rights

Sarmadax reserves the right to display the Work Product in its portfolio, case studies, and marketing materials unless Client requests in writing that specific materials remain confidential prior to project commencement.

### 4.5 No Transfer Before Full Payment

No IP assignment takes effect until all outstanding invoices under the applicable SOW are paid in full. Until then, Client receives only a limited, revocable license to use the deliverables.

### 5.1 Obligations

Each Party agrees to keep the other Party's non-public business information, technical data, and trade secrets ("Confidential Information") strictly confidential and not to disclose it to any third party without prior written consent, except to employees or contractors who have a need to know and are bound by confidentiality obligations at least as protective as this Agreement.

### 5.2 Exceptions

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly known through no breach of this Agreement; (b) was rightfully in the receiving Party's possession prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed without use of the Confidential Information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided prompt written notice is given.

### 5.3 Duration

Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for a period of **3 years**.
